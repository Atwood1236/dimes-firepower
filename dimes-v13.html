FAQ

What is Dimes?

Dimes is embedded credit infrastructure for onchain platforms. We let front-ends offer leverage, margining, and yield products to their users through a single integration, with no credit risk management required on their end.

How does onchain credit work with Dimes?

Dimes offers three core products that platforms can embed individually or together. Multiply provides up to 10x margin-funded directional leverage on assets not covered by perpetual futures, including prediction markets and liquid tokens. Firepower is native portfolio margining expanding to the top 50 most liquid tokens, freeing up capital without introducing leverage. Stream lets users maintain continuous synthetic exposure to assets like BTC or ETH, sized automatically against their existing holdings, without selling anything or managing positions manually.

Which platforms can integrate Dimes?

Any specialized onchain trading front-end. This includes trading terminals, mobile trading apps, token launchpads, wallets, and other platforms where users are actively deploying capital onchain. If your users trade, Dimes can amplify what they do with their capital.

How long does integration take?

Most teams go live within days. Dimes is a plug-and-play API designed to embed directly into your existing UI, so there's no need to rebuild your user experience or manage any credit operations.

What sets Dimes apart from other leverage solutions?

Three things. First, guaranteed liquidity: Dimes operates with a private counterparty under a guaranteed credit access agreement, ensuring that capital is always available to your users regardless of market conditions. Second, full risk outsourcing: all underwriting, monitoring, and liquidation management is handled by Dimes' risk engine, so your team never touches credit risk. Third, revenue sharing: platforms earn 30% of Dimes' revenues on every credit product their users access, turning leverage into a new revenue line with zero operational overhead.

What does integration look like for end users?

Users see leverage, margin, and streaming features natively inside the platform. There is no redirect, no separate account, and no additional KYC beyond what the platform already requires. From the user's perspective, it feels like a built-in feature.

How does revenue sharing work?

Platforms earn 30% of the fees generated every time a user borrows through Multiply, accesses margin through Firepower, or activates a stream. Revenue scales directly with user activity, and Dimes handles all billing and settlement. Your performance data is accessible at all times via your Partner Portal at partner.dimes.fi.
